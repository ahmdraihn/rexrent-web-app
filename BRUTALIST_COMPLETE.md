## 📐 Design Principles

### Brutalist Web Design
1. **Raw aesthetics** - No polish, no gradients (except accent)
2. **High contrast** - Black and white with neon accent
3. **Bold typography** - Large, uppercase, monospace
4. **Visible structure** - Thick borders, exposed grids
5. **Honest messaging** - Direct, no corporate speak
6. **Functional first** - Form follows function

### Applied Throughout
- All buttons have hard shadows
- All forms have thick borders
- All text is monospace
- All labels use [BRACKETS]
- Hover effects translate elements
- No rounded corners
- No subtle gradients (except accent hover)
